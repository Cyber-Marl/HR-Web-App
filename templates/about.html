Who We Are

STRATEGIC SYNERGY is a Human Resources Consulting firm, synchronizing strategy and talent to drive superior performance for our clients.

Our guiding principle is that our clients HR challenges are completely unique to their own organizational culture and our solutions must just be as unique and specifically tailored to their needs. So before any intervention, we seek to have an in-depth understanding of the needs to be addressed.

Solutions For A Dynamic World

There is no doubt about it, the world is changing faster than ever. And businesses are transforming to keep up, or keep ahead. No sector is immune.

It is a huge challenge for an organization to reinvent itself. Most transformations fail to deliver on their objectives. The root cause is an organization's inability to create internal alignment between structure, the people and the strategy.

True change is about people. It requires all employees to understand what a new strategy means for them. To be inspired by it.

Managing Consultant

Rodrick Bumhira

Rodrick is founder and Managing Consultant at Strategic Synergy. He held a number of Senior positions in Human Resources and has 26 years in HR having worked for FINHOLD/ZB Bank, INNSCOR AFRICA, Lorimak, Interfin Merchant Bank, Standard Chartered Bank and PriceWaterhouseCoopers.

He is currently Non-Executive Chairman at Original Technology and a Non-Executive Director at Sanctuary Insurance Company and Kreamorn Investments.
